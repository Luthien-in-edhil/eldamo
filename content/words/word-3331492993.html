Q. Minyarussa m. “First-russa”

A nickname given to S. Amras to distinguish him from his twin brother Amrod, since they were both known by the name Ambarussa “Top-russet” for their red hair (VT41/10). It is a compound of minya “first” and russa “red-haired”.
