√LIM root. “link, join; ?swift”

This root is attested with the glosses “link, join” (VT47/6) but it is also the likeliest root of S. lim, Q. linta “swift, quick”. It is conceivable the two meanings are related, as with English “fast” and “fasten”, both from Old English “fæst” (firmly fixed, steadfast).
