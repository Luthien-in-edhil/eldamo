N. Tol Fuin loc.

An island appearing in draft maps for the Lord of the Rings but not in the published version (TI/301), a combination of toll “island” and fuin “night”.

Conceptual Development: This island also appeared in draft maps for the Lord of the Rings (TI/298), but not appear in the main text.

References ✧ SMI; TII

Elements

toll “island, isle”
fuin “night, dead of night”
