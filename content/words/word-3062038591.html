Q. subjunctive grammar.

Reference ✧ VT42/33.3906 ✧ for example: lá karita i hamil mára alasaila (ná) “not to do (in this case) what you judge good (would be) unwise”, lá karita, navin, alasaila ná “not doing this would be (I think) unwise”, lá karita alasaila ké nauva “not doing this may be/prove unwise”
