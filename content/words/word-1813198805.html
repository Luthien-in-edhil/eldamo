Q. Yávien f.

First child of Nolondil, known only from a geneology chart on UT/210. Her name seems to be a compound of yávë “fruit” and the feminine suffix -ien.

Reference ✧ UT/210.0404

Elements

yávë “fruit”
-ien “feminine ending; -daughter”
