N. Taurost loc. “High City”

The name of the central citadel of Minas Tirith in Lord of the Rings drafts (WR/260). The name is glossed “High City” and the second element is clearly ost “city”. The first element might be taur² “mighty” (Ety/TÁWAR, TUR) or †taur³ “king” (Ety/TĀ), but I suspect the initial element got its sense from ON. tōra “lofty” (Ety/TĀ).
