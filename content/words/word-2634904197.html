Q. Atanalcar m. “*Man Glory”

Fourth child of Elros, known only from a geneology chart on UT/210. His name seems to be a compound of Atan “Man” and alcar “glory”.

Reference ✧ UT/210.0205

Elements

Atan “Man, (lit.) the Second (People)”
alcar “glory, splendour, radiance, brilliance”
