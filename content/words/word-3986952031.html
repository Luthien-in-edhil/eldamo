Q. Mairen f.

Third child of Tar-Amandil, known only from a geneology chart on UT/210. Her name might be a feminized form of maira “admirable”.

Reference ✧ UT/210.0403

Elements

maira “admirable, excellent, precious, splendid, sublime”
