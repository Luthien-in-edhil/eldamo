Taliska

A language of the Men of Beleriand from Tolkien’s Middle Period (1930-1950 in the terminology of this lexicon). As such, it may be considered a precursor to the languages of the Edain as Tolkien conceived of them after the publication of The Lord of the Rings. Not much information has been published about this language.
