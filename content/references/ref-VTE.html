Vinyar Tengwar Errata (VTE)

— Editors of Vinyar Tengwar; Dec. 6 2008; http://www.elvish.org/errata/VT-Errata.pdf

Corrections to Vinyar Tengwar articles. Only those items that impact Tolkien’s original writings are included. References are by issue corrected, with decimal values indicating the page number (first 2 digits) and the number of items corrected (last two digits).
