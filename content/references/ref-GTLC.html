Glǽmscrafu — Tolkien’s Linguistic Cellar (GTLC)

— Bertrand Bellet, Benjamin Babut; 2006-present; http://www.jrrvf.com/~glaemscrafu/

This site collects most of Tolkien’s major texts in his various languages, along with translations. I found it useful when working on my own analysis of the texts. Although the site does not analyze the texts in detail, having all the information in one place was helpful, and a few of the translations on the site are original.
