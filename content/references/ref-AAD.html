An Adunaic Dictionary (AAD)

— Carl Hostetter, Patrick Wynne; September 1992; Vinya Tengwar #25, pp. 8-26

An article in Vinya Tengwar #25 analyzing all the Adûnaic vocabulary published up through Sauron Defeated. Despite its age, the article is pretty comprehensive, missing only a few Adûnaic names published later in People of Middle-Earth. References are to page numbers in VT25.
